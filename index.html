About Me

My curiosity for how things work has brought me to this point.

I love what I do and I am constantly learning and trying to stay ahead of the ever-changing trends. I've had opportunities that have lead me across the country. I have worked in New York City as a Volunteer Coordinator for Google, designed Flash banner ads at an ad agency in Chicago, IL, and now I'm currently working as a Front-End Developer & UI Designer in Minneapolis, MN.

In my free time, I volunteer with Girl Develop It! and participate in community hack-a-thons. On the weekends, I like to order pizza, binge watch netflix, and have a coding night with my partner.
