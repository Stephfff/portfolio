- about the potter -

Ray Medeiros, Jr. started woodworking with his father, a master cabinet maker, when he was just nine years old. His love of woodworking has included reproductions of 17th and 18th century furniture as well as custom furniture pieces. Currently, he has returned to creating traditional crafts featuring Swedish and Japanese influences, such as wooden tools for culinary and pastry chefs (for use in both home and professional kitchens).
